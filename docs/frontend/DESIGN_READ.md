# M8 Design Read

Reading this as: a desktop-first Applied AI operations product for marketing,
game, and operations teams, with a calm cyber-enterprise language, restrained
motion, high information density, and explicit human-control states.

## Taste dials

- `DESIGN_VARIANCE: 4`: recognizable identity with conventional navigation.
- `MOTION_INTENSITY: 2`: feedback and state transitions only.
- `VISUAL_DENSITY: 7`: compact tables, timelines, forms, and analytical views.

The `design-taste-frontend` skill is applied contextually. Its brand, typography,
spacing, authentication, and empty-state guidance is useful. Its landing-page and
cinematic motion patterns are intentionally excluded from operational screens.

## Visual direction

Cyber Legends AI Operations Workspace combines quiet enterprise operations with a
controlled game-tech identity. The interface uses graphite and cool white surfaces,
signal cyan as the single brand accent, and semantic green, amber, red, and blue only
for states. Borders carry most grouping; cards are reserved for repeated resources,
dialogs, and focused tools.

The product uses a stable left navigation, compact headers, data tables, split detail
views, timelines, and explicit review actions. It avoids AI-purple gradients,
glassmorphism, decorative dashboards, oversized data-page titles, and hidden actions.
