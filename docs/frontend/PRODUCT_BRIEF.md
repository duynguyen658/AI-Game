# Product Brief

Cyber Legends AI Operations Workspace turns the M1-M7 backend into an internal
product for running and reviewing applied AI work. Marketing users create campaigns
and task inputs. Reviewers inspect outputs and make human decisions. Managers govern
prompts, experiments, providers, and business impact. Administrators monitor jobs,
alerts, health, and controlled actions.

## Product outcomes

- Complete a campaign workflow from brief to review and feedback.
- Run CSV, document, image, and storyboard tasks through authoritative APIs.
- Make sensitive decisions explicit and server-confirmed.
- Explain technical completion separately from human acceptance.
- Keep prompt, provider, cost, job, and correlation traceability visible.
- Support a deterministic 5-8 minute demo without real provider calls.

## Non-goals

No external publishing, generic workflow builder, vector memory UI, supervisor agent,
enterprise SSO, billing, native application, or browser-side provider SDK is included.
